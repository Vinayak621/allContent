NDVI plot

The NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index) is a numerical indicator that measures the health and density of vegetation. It provides valuable insights into the amount of green vegetation present in an area. The NDVI values range from 0 to 1, where higher values indicate healthier and denser vegetation.

In the generated plot, the x-axis represents the years from the start year to the end year provided by the user. The y-axis represents the NDVI values. Peaks in the plot indicate periods of high vegetation density, while dips indicate periods of lower vegetation density.
